About Me

My introduction

I'm a Freelance designer specializing in personal & e-commerce website design. I have been building websites most of my life, and enjoy learning about different types of businesses and how they operate. willpower.codes has really formed me as a developer, through clients with specific aspirations and clear direction always challenging me to learn more and venture into new platforms/tech!

I have strong background in using Adobe software: especially Illustrator, Photoshop & Premier. I am capable of producing both vector & raster designs for a multitude of applications around the internet and through printed media. Working with HD content to create awe-inspiring video content is one of my favorite jobs to do.

I have always been inquisitive about e-commerce web tech and worked with a number of different platforms including WordPress & Shopify/BigCommerce. I can code in many languages including: HTML, CSS, Liquid, JavaScript, Python and Java. I am very interested in finding new ways to implement fresh frameworks and technologies and stayed tuned to other designers to learn and collaborate.
